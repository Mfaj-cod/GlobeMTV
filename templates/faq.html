Buffering on channels ?

Cause: If you are using LAN (Ethernet) Cable please unplug behind the box and re-plug it in, and If you are using wi-fi go on Tv setting and disconnect the wi-fi and connect again and unplug the power cable behind the box and re-plug it in.
